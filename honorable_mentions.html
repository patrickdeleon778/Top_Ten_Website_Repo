Honorable Mentions

Nyan Neko Sugar Girls

"Nyan Neko Sugar Girls" is a web series consisting of animated shorts created by the artist and animator Jeremy "FancyFelix" Eldridge. The series features anthropomorphic cat-girls named Raku, Hitoshi, and Niya, who engage in bizarre and often absurd antics while living in a surreal, candy-colored world. The humor of the show is often surreal and absurd, with a focus on physical gags and wordplay. The series has gained a cult following for its unique animation style and strange sense of humor. However, it is important to note that the series contains adult themes and is not suitable for children.

Groovy! - Kokia || Cardcaptor Sakura

The first ending theme of "Cardcaptor Sakura" is called "Groovy!" and is performed by the Japanese singer Kokia. The song has a cheerful and upbeat sound with a mix of pop and rock elements. The visuals of the ending sequence feature a mix of scenes from the anime, with a focus on the main characters, Sakura Kinomoto and Li Syaoran. The sequence starts with Sakura and Li walking together, with Sakura looking back and smiling at the camera.

Bokura wa Ima no Naka de - μ's || Love Live!

The first opening theme of "Love Live!" is called "Bokura wa Ima no Naka de" and is performed by the Japanese idol group μ's (pronounced "muse"). The song has an upbeat and energetic sound with a mix of pop and rock elements.

Moonlight Densetsu || Sailor Moon

The first opening of Sailor Moon is called "Moonlight Densetsu" (which translates to "Moonlight Legend" in English). It was used for the first two seasons of the original Japanese anime series, which aired from 1992 to 1994.

Koi wa A La Mode || Tokyo Mew Mew

The first ending of Tokyo Mew Mew is a song called "Koi wa A La Mode" (Love is A La Mode) performed by the voice actresses of the main characters. The animation sequence features the Mew Mews in various poses, dancing and jumping around in a colorful and energetic setting. The song has a catchy and upbeat melody, with lyrics that talk about the joy and excitement of falling in love. As the song ends, we see the Mew Mews holding hands and smiling, suggesting the bonds of friendship and love that they have formed throughout their adventures.
