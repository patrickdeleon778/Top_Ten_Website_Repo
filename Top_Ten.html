Top Ten Anime Opening and Ending themes

#10

Mixed Nuts - Official Hige Dandism || Spy x Family

The first opening of "Spy x Family" is titled "ESPionage," and it features an upbeat and energetic song with a fast-paced rhythm. The opening begins with a shot of a city skyline at night, followed by a montage of the three main characters: Loid Forger, Yor Forger, and Anya Forger.

#9

Deal with the devil - Tia || Kakegurui

The first opening of "Kakegurui" is titled "Deal with the devil," and it features a dynamic and intense song with a strong rock beat. The opening begins with a shot of the main character, Yumeko Jabami, standing confidently against a backdrop of playing cards.

#8

A Cruel Angel's Thesis - Yoko Takahashi || Evangelion

The first opening of "Neon Genesis Evangelion" is titled "A Cruel Angel's Thesis," and it features an iconic and memorable song with a fast-paced beat and catchy lyrics. The opening begins with a series of abstract and surreal images, including flashing lights, swirling colors, and distorted shapes.

#7

Kuusou Mesorogi || Mirai Nikki

The first opening of "Mirai Nikki," also known as "Future Diary," is titled "Kuusou Mesorogiwi," and it features an intense and fast-paced song with a strong rock beat. The opening begins with a shot of the main character, Yukiteru Amano, holding his phone, which serves as his "Future Diary" and predicts the future.

#6

Lost in Paradise - AKLO || JUJUTSU KAISEN

The first ending of "Jujutsu Kaisen" is titled "Lost in Paradise" and it features a catchy and upbeat song with a mix of pop, rock, and electronic elements. The ending begins with a shot of the main character, Yuji Itadori, standing against a colorful and abstract background.

#5

Tank! - Seatbelts || Cowboy Bebop

The first opening of "Cowboy Bebop" is titled "Tank!" and it features a jazzy and upbeat instrumental track with a mix of brass and percussion instruments. The opening begins with a shot of the spaceship "Bebop," which serves as the main setting for the series.

#4

Wild Side - ALI || Beastars

The first opening of "Beastars" is titled "Wild Side," and it features a dynamic and energetic song with a mix of rock and electronic elements. The opening begins with a shot of the main character, Legoshi, a timid and gentle gray wolf.

#3

Tablet - TOOBOE || Chainsaw Man Ending 4

"Tablet" is a catchy and upbeat song with a mix of electronic and pop elements. The song features playful and dreamy vocals, with lyrics that touch on themes of loneliness, self-reflection, and finding comfort in technology and virtual communication.

#2

Hawatari Niku Centi || Chainsaw Man Ending 3

"Hawatari Niku Centi" is the second ending theme song for the anime adaptation of "Chainsaw Man," performed by the Japanese rock band Maximum The Hormone. The song features a fast-paced and aggressive sound, with heavy guitar riffs and energetic drumming.

#1

KICK BACK - Kenshi Yonezu || Chainsaw Man Opening

"Kick Back" is a song performed by the Japanese singer-songwriter Kenshi Yonezu, which serves as the opening theme for the first season of the "Chainsaw Man" anime adaptation. The song has a melodic and catchy sound with a mix of electronic and acoustic elements.
